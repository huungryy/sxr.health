# FAQ

Frequently asked questions

1. What services does Southeast X-Ray offer?

We provide mobile diagnostic imaging services including X-rays, EKGs, and ultrasound to clinics, urgent care centers, long-term care facilities, and VA programs.

2. How quickly can results be delivered?

Most diagnostic results are delivered within 24 hours, and STAT requests are expedited even faster through our PACS system and radiologist network.

3. Do you offer on-site mobile imaging?

Yes. Our mobile technicians travel directly to your location—no need for patients to visit a hospital or imaging center. We bring certified equipment and technicians to you.

4. Is your equipment certified and compliant?

All our equipment is state-of-the-art, regularly calibrated, and meets all state and federal compliance standards for mobile diagnostic services.

5. How do we schedule a mobile visit?

You can call us directly or submit a request through our website. Our dispatch team coordinates with your facility’s schedule to ensure seamless service delivery.

6. Are your radiologists board-certified?

Yes. All imaging interpretations are completed by board-certified radiologists to ensure diagnostic accuracy and clinical confidence.

7. Do you serve government or veteran facilities?

Absolutely. Southeast X-Ray proudly partners with VA clinics and other government healthcare providers across the region.
